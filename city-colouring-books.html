WHY THESE BOOKS

Not a typical colouring book.

These books are made for people who want something more considered than generic colouring pages. Every illustration is built around structure, balance and bold visual rhythm, so the finished page feels calm, modern and satisfying.

Choose one as a thoughtful creative gift, a screen-free evening activity, or a way to reconnect with a city you love.

Designed for adultsclean lines, city details, architecture, travel and mindful colouring
BAUHAUS STYLE

Geometry, balance and cities in line.

Inspired by Bauhaus principles, the illustrations use clean shapes, strong composition and architectural forms. The pages avoid random clutter and keep enough open space to make colouring enjoyable.

The result is a graphic travel colouring book style that feels grown-up, design-led and easy to start.
